Examples

Some example uses for Universe.js. Try these live demos in your own browser.

Having problems viewing the demos? Make sure your browser is WebGL compatable.

  • Model the GPS constellation

    Shows all satellites of the GPS constellation, their orbit paths and their ground paths. Click and drag to move the camera point-of-view. Scroll to zoom in and out.

  • View some space junk

    Model locations of space debris. See where items are, and how they change through time.

  • Eccentric orbit satellites

    Model and simulate multiple satellites with varying orbit paths. Similar to the GPS example, but shows how any orbit can be modeled.

  • Ground points

    Take your "dots on a map" to a whole new level. 3D viewing gives a new perspective of your data over traditional 2D. Dots can be selected, which would allow actions to be taken on a click event.
