Examples

Some example uses for Universe.js. Try these live demos in your own browser.

  • Model the GPS constellation

    Shows all satellites of the GPS constellation, their orbit paths and their ground paths. Click and drag to move the camera point-of-view. Scroll to zoom in and out.

    You will need to log in (it's free) to the Reveal® website to create this demo.
  • Basic Earth & Moon example

    Let's start at the beginning. Earth, moon, a space vehicle and ground dots. See how it all works, for real - in your browser. Feel free to view the source code.

     

  • Eccentric orbit satellites

    Model and simulate multiple satellites with varying orbit paths. Create multiple sensors. Show projections and footprints. Similar to the GPS example, but shows how any orbit can be modeled.

    You will need to log in (it's free) to the Reveal® website to create this demo.
  • Ground points

    Take your "dots on a map" to a whole new level. 3D viewing gives a new perspective of your data over traditional 2D. This example shows retrieving data through an "AJAX" call.

    Select points to see how click events can be captured, and additional data displayed.

Having problems viewing the demos? Make sure your browser is WebGL compatable.
